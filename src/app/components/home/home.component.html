Exploring Ancient Ruins in Athens, Greece
Our final destination took us to the ancient city of Athens, Greece. Exploring the Acropolis with kids in tow was like walking through a history lesson. They were amazed by the towering columns of the Parthenon, and we spent time imagining what life was like for the ancient Greeks. Athens also offered us a balance of history and relaxation — we'd spend mornings exploring ruins and afternoons enjoying delicious Greek cuisine at outdoor tavernas. The kids became fans of Greek yogurt with honey, and we indulged in family meals with fresh seafood and local dishes. One evening, we took a sunset cruise along the Aegean Sea, a beautiful and peaceful end to our family adventure.

5-Day Budget Itinerary for Bali
This 5-day itinerary for Bali offers a budget-friendly way to explore the island's natural beauty, culture, and beaches. Highlights include: Day 1: Explore Ubud's Monkey Forest, Ubud Market, and Tegalalang Rice Terraces. Stay in a budget guesthouse and enjoy local food. Day 2: Visit temples like Tirta Empul and Goa Gajah, and take the Campuhan Ridge Walk. End with a traditional Balinese dance performance. Day 3: Hike Mount Batur for a sunrise view and relax in the Toya Devasya Hot Springs. Day 4: Enjoy a beach day at Kuta or Seminyak, try surfing, and visit Uluwatu Temple for a sunset and Kecak Dance. Day 5: Take a boat trip to Nusa Penida and explore scenic spots like Kelingking Beach and Broken Beach. Budget Tips: Stay in guesthouses, eat at local warungs, rent scooters, and book tours locally to keep costs down.
